
# 🌙 Sleep Cycle Alarm & Neon Mini-Game

A premium, production-ready Flutter application designed to optimize your wake-up routine. Calculate the perfect time to wake up based on 90-minute sleep cycles and dismiss your alarm with a high-octane neon driving challenge built on the Flame engine.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Mathematical Sleep Engine**: Calculates exactly 6 wake-up windows based on your bedtime, optimized for REM cycles.
*   **Flame Mini-Game**: Survive a 30-second driving mission to dismiss the alarm—no more "accidental" snoozing.
*   **Premium Aesthetics**: A dark mode (#121212) interface with glassmorphism, glowing neon purple accents, and fluid animations.
*   **Dynamic UX**: Real-time "Recommended for you" highlighting and a sticky bottom CTA.

## 🎮 How to Play (Alarm Mission)

1.  **Select a Time**: Pick one of the calculated sleep cycles.
2.  **Survive**: Once the alarm triggers, steer your neon car using tap zones (left/right).
3.  **Avoid Hits**: Crashing into obstacles resets the 30-second countdown.
4.  **Wake Up**: Complete the 30s mission to successfully dismiss the alarm.
